Rivers Lab Research Workflow

Educational Psychology and Learning Support

Self-Regulation → Evidence → Modelling → Support → Improvement
1
Context

Student Learning Activity

Coursework, assignments, study routines, attendance, and classroom participation.

2
Constructs

Self-Regulation and Metacognition

Planning, monitoring, evaluation, adjustment, and executive-function behaviour.

3
Evidence

Survey and Behavioural Data

Self-report instruments, LMS records, assignment timing, and academic outcomes.

4
Processing

Data Preparation

Cleaning, coding, linking, structuring, and preparation for quantitative analysis.

5
Analysis

Statistical Modelling

Regression, factor analysis, path analysis, and structural equation modelling.

6
Output

Learning Support Insight

Practical evidence for intervention, course design, advising, and student feedback.

7
Improvement

Teaching and Support Adaptation

Improved instruction, targeted support, and refined institutional practice.

Evidence loop Student learning activity is converted into interpretable evidence, then returned to teaching, advising, and support design.
Learning evidence loop — each cycle informs better support
